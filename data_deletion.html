Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your personal data from [Your App Name], follow these steps:

  • Login to your account
  • Go to **Settings > Account > Delete My Account**
  • Confirm your deletion request

Alternative Method

You can also request account deletion by contacting us on Facebook:https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1KS5LS4mW5/. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

Important Notice

Once your data is deleted, it **cannot be recovered**. Please back up any important data before proceeding.
